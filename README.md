# Amazon Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes Amazon product sales performance using interactive visuals and time-based KPIs. The report helps track Year-to-Date (YTD) and Quarter-to-Date (QTD) sales, product sales trends, customer review activity, and category-level performance. The dashboard is designed to provide a clear view of product performance across different categories and time periods, helping identify top-performing products, seasonal trends, and areas for business focus.

## Short Description / Purpose

The Amazon Sales Dashboard is a dynamic Power BI report created to analyze and visualize Amazon product sales data in an interactive way. It focuses on key performance indicators such as YTD Sales, QTD Sales, Products Sold, and Customer Reviews, along with sales trends by month, week, category, and top-performing products. This dashboard is useful for business analysts, e-commerce professionals, and decision-makers who want to track sales performance, identify high-performing categories, and gain insights into customer review activity.

## Objectives

* Monitor overall sales performance using YTD and QTD metrics
* Track product demand and review activity over time
* Compare category-wise sales performance
* Identify top-performing products based on sales and reviews
* Enable users to filter performance by product category

## Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main data visualization platform used to design and build the interactive sales dashboard.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation of raw Amazon sales data before analysis.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used to create calculated measures such as YTD Sales, QTD Sales, Products Sold, and YTD Reviews.
* 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Used to establish relationships between the sales data and date table, enabling accurate time-based analysis and cross-filtering across visuals.
* 📁 **File Format** – `.pbit` for the Power BI template file and `.png` / screenshots for dashboard preview images in GitHub.

## Data Source

**Source:** Kaggle – Amazon Sales Dataset

The dashboard is built using an Amazon sales dataset sourced from Kaggle. The dataset contains product-level and sales-related information such as product categories, product descriptions, prices, number of reviews, shipment details, and order dates. This structured dataset was used to analyze sales performance across time periods, compare category-level contributions, identify top-selling products, and evaluate customer engagement through review activity.

## Key KPIs

* **YTD Sales** – Total sales generated from the beginning of the year to the selected date
* **QTD Sales** – Total sales generated from the beginning of the quarter to the selected date
* **YTD Products Sold** – Total number of products sold year-to-date
* **YTD Reviews** – Total number of customer reviews accumulated year-to-date

## Dashboard Features

* KPI cards for YTD Sales, QTD Sales, Products Sold, and Reviews
* Monthly sales trend analysis
* Weekly sales performance tracking
* Category-wise sales comparison
* Top 5 products by YTD Sales
* Top 5 products by YTD Reviews
* Interactive product category filtering

## Key Business Insights

The dashboard reveals clear patterns in sales performance across both monthly and weekly periods, making it easier to identify peak sales windows as well as lower-performing periods. By breaking sales down at the product category level, the report highlights which categories contribute the most to overall revenue and where business performance is concentrated. The Top 5 Products by YTD Sales visual helps identify the highest-performing products and potential revenue drivers, while the Top 5 Products by YTD Reviews visual provides insight into customer engagement, product popularity, and review activity. In addition, the use of YTD and QTD KPI tracking allows users to monitor both short-term and long-term sales performance trends in a structured and measurable way.

## Business Value

This dashboard provides business users with an interactive reporting solution for monitoring product sales performance and customer engagement. It can be used to track sales trends over time, identify top-performing product categories and products, understand customer interaction through review volume, and support both category-level and product-level decision-making. The use of KPI cards, trend visuals, and interactive filters improves reporting visibility and helps convert raw sales data into actionable business insights.
